About Me

Organized systems. Reliable execution. Measurable results.

For seven years, I've worked in government service as an Environment Management Specialist, providing executive and administrative support while coordinating environmental and community development projects. My day-to-day spans documentation and report preparation, event management, stakeholder coordination, and business operations — the kind of work that keeps an office, and a project, running smoothly.

Outside of government work, I'm also a Virtual Assistant for Etsy and TikTok Shop sellers, where I handle product listing, SEO optimization, product research, and image editing — helping small e-commerce businesses look polished and get found.

What ties it all together is that I genuinely enjoy organizing information, streamlining processes, and solving operational problems. I like supporting executives, helping businesses grow, learning new tools, and delivering accurate, high-quality work — every time.
